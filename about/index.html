About Nathan Gabriel

Nathan Gabriel is a professional Australian musician and live performer, specializing in electric and acoustic guitar. With over 10 years of experience, he delivers engaging performances that adapt to any venue or audience — from intimate private functions to large festivals and pub gigs.

Musical Style & Performance

Blending original music with crowd-favourite covers, Nathan Gabriel's sets are versatile and dynamic. He can create a full-bodied sound for energetic shows or a stripped-back acoustic experience for intimate settings, using adaptable hardware and instruments tailored to each event.

Live Performances

Experience & Highlights

  • Over 10 years performing across Victoria at weddings, pubs, festivals, corporate events, and private functions.
  • Albums released on Spotify, Apple Music, and Bandcamp.
  • Featured in local press and community events throughout Sunbury and surrounding areas.
  • Hundreds of satisfied clients with positive reviews and repeat bookings.
